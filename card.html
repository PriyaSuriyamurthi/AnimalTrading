Deer

Deer are browsers, and feed primarily on leaves. They have small, unspecialized stomachs by ruminant standards, and high nutrition requirements.

  • Scientific Name: Cervidae
  • Average Length: 6 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 25 years
  • Habitat: temperate, tropical, polar

Deer constitute the second most diverse family after bovids.Though of a similar build, deer are strongly distinguished from antelopes by their antlers, which are temporary and regularly regrown unlike the permanent horns of bovids. Characteristics typical of deer include long, powerful legs, a diminutive tail and long ears.Deer exhibit a broad variation in physical proportions.
